Myspace

MySpace was originally the site a user could make a page all about them. The user could edit everything on their page, from their profile picture to the background of their page. However with the dawn of facebook came the decline of MySpace. MySpace still exists, however it is mainly used bands to get their music out there. Myspace is a social networking website offering an interactive, user-submitted network of friends, personal profiles, blogs, groups, photos,music, and videos. It is headquartered in Beverly Hills, California. It was founded on August 1st, 2003. Myspace was then acquired by News Corporation in July of 2005 for $580 million. From 2005 to 2008, Myspace was the largest social networking site in the world. It even surpassed google as the most visited website in June 2006. However, in April 2008, Myspace was overtaken by Facebook in the number of unique worldwide visitors, and was surpassed in the number of unique U.S. visitors in May 2009.

Facebook

While not as customizeable as MySpace, FaceBook introduced a more structured and easier to use platfrom. Facebook was created by Mark Zuckerburg, and launched on Febuary 4th, 2004, while he was still a student at Havard University. While still being able to customize the user's individual profile, there is also the instant message option to keep in contact with friends and family. Another key feature that had the sole purpose of attracting teen and young adult users is the relationship status. FaceBook in general was made to target a young audience.

Instagram Checkmate.

Instagram was launched on October 6th, 2010. It was originally developed by Kevin Systrom and Mike Krieger of Burbn INC. It was later bought up by Facebook in 2012 for a price of $1 billion worth of cash and stock. Instagram is a mobile, desktop and web-based photo-sharing app and service that allows users to share their photos and videos to either the whole public, or just privately to your approved followers. Once uploaded, the user can apply a number of filters to their photos and videos. Users can also add their location through geotags, and also add hashtags to their comments. There are new Instagram users starting new profiles almost daily, currently the number of Instagram users are in the 600 million range.
